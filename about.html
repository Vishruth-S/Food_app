ABOUT US

Eatssy is the easy way to get the food you love delivered.

Who we are

Eatssy is a technology company that connects people with the best in their cities. We do this by empowering local businesses and in turn, generate new ways for people to earn, work and live. We started by facilitating door-to-door delivery, but we see this as just the beginning of connecting people with possibility — easier evenings, happier days, bigger savings accounts, wider nets and stronger communities.

Partners

Delivering with Eatssy, you get flexibility and financial stability. Eatssy for a living or for a goal, all on your schedule and on your own terms.

Be a part of our story

We're always looking for great folks to join us on our mission. If you want to be a part of our story, we'd love to chat.
